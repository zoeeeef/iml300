Level 1-2

At levels 1-2, you are often calm, content, stable, and relaxed. You feel grounded and focused. Normal breathing and neutral body.

Level 3-4

At leavels 3-4, you are experiencing mild-moderate depression and/or anxiety. You are distracted and your thoughts are scattered. You may experience feeling wired or wound tight with racing thoughts. You may experience discomfort and you may be dispondent.

Level 5-6

At levels 5-6, you are experiencing very high levels of depression and/or anxiety. You may feel high urges to SH and you may have SI. You may be sobbing or feel completely numb. You are experiencing high levels of discomfort and the inability to rest your body and mind. You may experience intense racing thought or be dissociated. You could also experience de-personalization and irregular breathing and heartbeat.

Level 7

At level 7, you are often in danger. You are experiencing severe anxiety and/or depression. You may be engaging in SH and you may go beyond just SI and could potentially have a plan or intent. Your thoughts can be uncontrollable and you may experience intolerable malaise. You may be sobbing or screaming, or you could be completely catatonic and unresponsive. You may be completely dissociated and feel you are experiencing an out of body experience.
